ParentRound.com is an online platform where you can say thanks to your school.

Parent Round makes it easy for parents to share their appreciation with their child’s school, teachers, and other parents. Easily find your school, add a teacher or parent’s name and select from a pre-populated list of appreciative comments to express your thanks!

Teachers are not only special to your students, they too appreciate your child’s presence. In this incredibly powerful video by Community ISD, we see the profound impact of telling a child you believe in them.
